About Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya

Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV), formerly University of Indore, is a premiere university in Central India, located in the heart of Madhya Pradesh. It is a premier institution of higher learning, renowned for its academic excellence, innovative research, and vibrant campus life. It has completed 60 years of establishment, educational commitment and services. It is the first University of Madhya Pradesh which has been accredited with "A+" grade by NAAC.

It is catering to the educational needs on one hand to the most industrially and infrastructurally developed district of MP, Indore and on the other hand to the tribal and rural backward districts of the state.

DAVV university's unwavering pursuit of knowledge and excellence continues to inspire and shape the future of countless young minds through well qualified human resources and value based education. New horizons and keep moving ahead on the path of excellence, innovation and enlightenment.

About International Institute of Professional Studies

International Institute of Professional Studies (IIPS), a pioneer institute under Devi AhilyaVishwavidyalaya to provide a new dimension to professional education. IIPS is a realm where education meets innovation, and dreams take flight. The Institute has been providing exceptional opportunities to the students for the past 32 years. IIPS stands as a paragon of academic and industry excellence and a beacon of innovation in professional studies. The Institute is located in the sprawling Takshashila campus of the University. The lush green campus of the IIPS Includes an academic complex of classrooms, seminar room, an auditorium, a well-equipped library, computer labs, development center, and administrative offices.

Since its inception, the Institute has been dedicated to fostering an environment where scholarly rigor, professional development, and personal growth converge harmoniously. The institute offers a diverse range of programs, meticulously designed to address the evolving demands of the professional world. This martinet institute offers integrated post-graduate courses in the fields of computer and management which students join directly after schooling. In addition to that institute serves specialized courses in advertisement, tourism and public relations management as well. The institute provides a rigorous, intellectually challenging curriculum with flexibility to allow students to tailor their education to meet their professional and personal interests and goals. It provides a collaborative learning environment that weaves technology and management into everyday life. With the quality in the content, scope and professionalism of its cross-disciplinary programs, IIPS is one of the best centers of technical and management education in central India.

About the Conference

The United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) represent a global call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure prosperity for all by 2030. Despite significant progress, the complexity and interconnected nature of these goals necessitate innovative, cross sectoral approaches. There is a need to look into the current practices, approaches, challenges and future strategies adopted across the globe. This conference aims to bring together experts from various disciplines to analyze, discuss, deliberate on relevant issues and generate new strategies for achieving the SDGs.
